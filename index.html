Baltimore Energy & Infrastructure

Baltimore Transportation & Infrastructure: Assessing Urban Heat Vulnerability in Baltimore Neighborhoods to Inform Transportation Resiliency Planning Efforts


This NASA DEVELOP Spring 2024 project generated maps outlining bus routes and infrastructure that were at the most risk of experiencing the heat effects of climate change. This analysis was conducted using NASA DEVELOP's UHEAT tool via Google Earth Engine JavaScript API and R Studio.
